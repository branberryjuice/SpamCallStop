About SpamCallStop

SpamCallStop helps everyday people cut down on the spam and scam calls that come from having their phone number listed and sold online. We're a U.S. company, and we built this because the robocall problem is real, exhausting, and especially hard on older folks who get targeted the most.

What we do

Most spam calls start the same way: a data-broker or people-search site has your phone number and sells it. We find the sites that list your number, send opt-out and removal requests on your behalf as your authorized agent, and then keep checking month after month so your number stays off. Fewer listings means fewer calls.

How it works

You start with a free scan to see where your number shows up. If you choose to become a member, we go to work sending removals, give you a dashboard to follow the progress, and keep monitoring so listings that reappear get taken down again. There's nothing to install and nothing complicated to manage.

Our promise

If we haven't removed your number from every site we found within 90 days, we keep working at no extra cost until we do. You can cancel anytime, and your free scan is always free.

What we're not

We're honest about our limits. We're a phone-number removal service, not a law firm, and we can't promise that every single site cooperates or that every call stops. Some public records can't be removed, and results vary from person to person. We never sell your information, and we only use it to do the removals you ask for.
